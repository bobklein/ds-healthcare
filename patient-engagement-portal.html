FAQ

Common questions about patient portals.

Why build custom instead of using the EHR's portal?

EHR vendor portals are built for compliance, not engagement. Custom portals are designed around patient needs with mobile-first UX, resulting in higher adoption and better outcomes. You can integrate with the EHR without being limited by its UI.

Do you build native mobile apps or web apps?

We build native iOS and Android apps for the best mobile experience, plus a responsive web portal. Patients can use whichever they prefer with the same features and data.

How do you integrate with our EHR?

We have production experience with PointClickCare, Epic, Gehrimed, and Netsmart. The portal pulls patient data, syncs scheduling, and routes messages through your existing EHR.

How long does it take to build a patient portal?

Typical patient portal projects take 4-8 months depending on scope, EHR integrations, and feature complexity. We can deliver MVP functionality faster and iterate from there.

How do you ensure HIPAA compliance?

HIPAA compliance is built in from day one—encryption, secure authentication, audit logging, and BAA-ready infrastructure. We've been doing healthcare software since 2007.
