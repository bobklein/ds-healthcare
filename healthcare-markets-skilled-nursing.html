Market Challenges

The pressures facing skilled nursing facilities

PDPM Revenue Optimization

PDPM reimbursement depends on accurate MDS assessments. Missed diagnoses and coding errors leave millions on the table. Most facilities don't have the time or expertise to optimize every assessment.

Five-Star Rating Pressure

Quality measures directly impact reimbursement, referrals, and reputation. Meeting quality thresholds requires real-time visibility into measure performance and proactive intervention.

MDS Coordinator Burden

MDS assessments take 3-4 hours each. Coordinators are overwhelmed with paperwork while trying to ensure accuracy. Burnout and turnover compound the problem.

Staffing Shortages

The nursing shortage hits SNFs hardest. Facilities need to deliver quality care with fewer staff-which requires technology that extends clinician capacity.

Hospital Readmissions

Hospital transfers are expensive and affect quality ratings. SNFs in value-based arrangements need to keep residents in place when clinically appropriate.

Value-Based Care Transition

SNF VBP, ACO partnerships, and MA contracts are shifting economics from volume to value. Technology infrastructure must support this transition.
