Healthcare UX Design

Design That Reduces Friction and Improves Outcomes

Poor UX costs healthcare organizations millions in lost productivity and errors. We design interfaces that clinicians actually want to use—reducing cognitive load, preventing mistakes, and improving the patient experience.

Our Approach

What is Healthcare UX?

Strategic integration of user research, UX design, and human factors to optimize the health journey.

User Research

Understanding how patients and providers interact with digital healthcare solutions through interviews, observations, and usability testing.

Interviews, observations, usability testing

UX Design

Tailoring digital solutions to optimize patient experiences—wireframes, prototypes, and design systems that reduce complexity.

Wireframes, prototypes, design systems

Human Factors

Designing user-friendly environments and devices that consider cognitive and physical aspects to support healthcare professionals.

IEC 62366, error prevention, accessibility
