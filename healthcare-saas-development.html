FAQ

Common questions about healthcare SaaS development.

How long does it take to build a healthcare SaaS MVP?

Typical MVP timelines are 4-6 months depending on complexity and integration requirements. We can scope a more specific timeline during discovery.

Do you help with HIPAA compliance?

Yes. HIPAA compliance is built into our architecture from day one—encryption, access controls, audit logging, and BAA-ready infrastructure. We've been doing this since 2007.

Can you integrate with multiple EHR systems?

Yes. We have production experience with PointClickCare, Epic, Gehrimed, Netsmart, and custom EHRs. Multi-EHR integration is often a key SaaS differentiator.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes. We offer ongoing development, maintenance, and support. Most of our clients continue working with us post-launch as they scale and add features.

What about HITRUST certification?

We can architect for HITRUST readiness and support the certification process. This is increasingly important for enterprise healthcare sales.
