The Challenge

Clinicians spending more time on paperwork than patients.

The math was brutal: clinicians spent 45 minutes documenting each visit while the visit itself might only last 30 minutes. Documentation had become the primary activity, with patient care squeezed into the remaining time.

This wasn't just an efficiency problem-it was a burnout problem and a capacity problem. Organizations couldn't see more patients because their clinicians were drowning in documentation. Staff turnover increased as clinicians left for less paperwork-heavy roles. And the documentation burden only increased as compliance requirements grew more complex.

The challenge: reduce documentation burden dramatically while maintaining (or improving) documentation quality and compliance. Generic transcription tools didn't work-they produced text that still required extensive editing and didn't integrate with clinical workflows.

The Solution

Ambient AI documentation built for clinical reality.

HealthContext.AI captures clinical conversations and automatically generates compliant documentation-but it's far more than transcription. The platform understands clinical context, structures information according to documentation standards, and integrates directly with EHR workflows.

The AI doesn't just listen; it comprehends. It recognizes clinical terminology, identifies relevant diagnoses, and structures the documentation according to the specific requirements of the visit type-whether that's an OASIS assessment, MDS section, or standard progress note.

Clinicians review and approve the generated documentation rather than creating it from scratch. What once took 45 minutes now takes 5 minutes-and the resulting documentation is often more complete and consistent than manually created notes.
