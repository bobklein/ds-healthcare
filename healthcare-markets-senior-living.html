Today's residents and their families expect connected, personalized experiences. Communities that deliver them win occupancy and referrals.

Resident Engagement

Keeping residents active, connected, and engaged across different care levels and cognitive abilities. Generic solutions don't account for the diversity of your population.

Family Communication

Digital family portal solutions

Workflow automation

Community Types

Solutions for every senior living model.

CCRCs / Life Plan Communities

Unified platforms that track residents across the full continuum-from independent living through skilled nursing-with seamless transitions and consistent family communication.

Assisted Living

Engagement platforms, family portals, and care documentation tools designed for the assisted living care model. Balance independence with appropriate support.

Memory Care

Specialized solutions for memory care communities-family communication becomes even more critical, and technology must be designed for residents with cognitive impairment.

Independent Living

Resident engagement platforms that help active seniors stay connected, participate in community activities, and maintain independence while building community connections.
