The Challenge

Skilled nursing facilities needed 24/7 clinical coverage without unsustainable staffing costs.

CommuniCare Health Services, one of the nation's largest post-acute care providers, faced a challenge common across the industry: residents needed around-the-clock access to clinical assessment, but staffing constraints made traditional models unsustainable.

The consequences were significant. Unnecessary hospital transfers drove up costs and disrupted continuity of care. After-hours clinical questions went unanswered until morning. Families worried about their loved ones during night shifts. And facilities struggled to provide the level of responsiveness that residents deserved.

They needed a solution that could extend clinical reach without proportionally extending payroll-technology that augmented their care teams rather than replacing them.

The Solution

A complete virtual care platform built from the ground up.

We didn't implement an off-the-shelf telehealth solution. We built NeverAlone as a custom platform designed specifically for the post-acute care environment-addressing the unique clinical, operational, and regulatory requirements of skilled nursing facilities.

The platform provides 24/7 access to clinical assessment through video visits with licensed practitioners. But it goes beyond simple video calls. NeverAlone integrates with facility EHRs, supports care coordination workflows, and enables treatment-in-place for conditions that would otherwise result in hospital transfers.

Most importantly, we operate what we built. This isn't a handoff-Digital Scientists runs the NeverAlone platform in production, serving real patients every day across seven states.

How We Did It

The complete value chain in action.

NeverAlone demonstrates our complete 8-step approach-from initial data foundation through ongoing operational partnership.

1

Data Foundation

Built data pipelines integrating clinical information from multiple EHR systems across facilities. Created unified patient views enabling consistent care regardless of location.

2

Responsible AI Build

Developed clinical decision support with full transparency. Every recommendation traces to clinical evidence, supporting (not replacing) clinician judgment.

3

EHR Integration

Bidirectional integration with PointClickCare and other systems. Virtual visit documentation flows directly into patient records-no duplicate entry, no gaps.

4

Co-Design

Built alongside clinical staff who would use the platform daily. Workflows designed around how care is actually delivered, not how we assumed it should be.

5

Implementation

Phased rollout from pilot facility to multi-state deployment. On-site go-live support at each new location. Issues resolved in real-time, not through support tickets.

6

Change Management

Training programs for facility staff. Super-user identification and support. Addressed resistance by demonstrating value-staff became advocates when they saw residents benefit.

7

Ongoing Partnership

We didn't hand off and disappear. Digital Scientists continues to operate the NeverAlone platform, continuously improving based on clinical feedback and operational data.

8

Outcome Guarantee

Success measured in patient outcomes-96% treat-in-place rate, 32% cost reduction, 26,000+ patients served. These aren't marketing claims; they're operational metrics we track daily.
