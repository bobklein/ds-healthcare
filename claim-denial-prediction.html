The Problem

You're submitting claims you know will deny—you just don't know which ones.

Every healthcare organization has a denial rate. Most track it, try to reduce it, and accept some percentage as unavoidable. But here's the uncomfortable truth: many of those denials were predictable before submission.

The patterns exist in your data. Certain payer + code + facility + timing combinations deny at 40%, 60%, or 80% rates. Authorization requirements that are frequently missed. Documentation gaps that trigger the same denial reasons over and over.

Denial prediction AI learns these patterns from your history and applies them to claims before submission. High-risk claims get flagged with specific predicted reasons and recommended fixes. Your team can intervene before the denial happens—when it's easiest to fix.

The difference from Claim Triage: Triage identifies claims that need follow-up after submission. Denial Prediction catches problems before submission. Together, they create a complete prevention-and-recovery system.
