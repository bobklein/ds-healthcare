FAQ

Common questions about healthcare predictive analytics.

What is predictive analytics in healthcare?

Predictive analytics uses historical data, statistical algorithms, and machine learning to forecast future outcomes. Unlike reporting that shows what happened, predictive analytics identifies what will happen—enabling proactive interventions.

When should we choose custom over off-the-shelf?

Custom makes sense when you need deep EHR integration, your population is unique, you want predictions in workflows (not dashboards), your scale justifies it ($50M+ revenue), or generic tools have failed.

What ROI can we expect?

Verified outcomes: $10M+ RAF improvements, revenue cycles 45→28 days, denial rates 12%→4%, 25% readmission reduction. All financial analyst calculated, not vendor estimates.

How long does implementation take?

Assessment: ~1 month ($15-25K). Full implementation: 6-12 months. Our 100% production rate compares to 33% for internal builds that average 18+ months.

What EHR systems do you integrate with?

Production integrations with PointClickCare, Epic, Gehrimed, Elation, Acclivity, and custom EHRs. Bidirectional, workflow-embedded—insights appear where clinicians work.
