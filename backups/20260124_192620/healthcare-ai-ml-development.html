FAQ

Common questions about healthcare AI development.

How long does it take to build a healthcare AI solution?

Typical timelines are 3-6 months for initial production deployment, depending on data quality, integration complexity, and use case. We can scope more specifically during discovery.

Do we need our own data to build custom AI?

It depends on the use case. Some AI applications can leverage foundation models with minimal custom training. Others benefit significantly from training on your specific data. We assess this during discovery.

How do you ensure AI reliability and safety?

We implement responsible AI practices—explainability, bias monitoring, human-in-the-loop design, and comprehensive testing. Clinical AI requires additional validation beyond typical software.

Can you integrate AI with our existing EHR?

Yes. We have production experience with PointClickCare, Epic, Gehrimed, and Netsmart. Bidirectional integration is often critical for AI adoption—data flows in, insights flow back.

What ROI can we expect from healthcare AI?

Verified outcomes include $10M+ PDPM recovered, 45→5 min documentation, and 96% treat-in-place rates. We help you identify and measure ROI during discovery.
