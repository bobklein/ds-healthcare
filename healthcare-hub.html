Why Healthcare Is Different

Generic AI tools weren't built for your environment.

Healthcare isn't a horizontal market where you can drop in a generic tool and expect results. You have compliance requirements other industries don't face. EHR integrations that touch everything. Reimbursement rules that change how software needs to work.

That's why research shows custom solutions deliver 2x higher success rates than generic tools in healthcare. The organizations crossing the "GenAI Divide" are buying custom, learning-capable systems-not deploying ChatGPT wrappers.

What makes healthcare different:

  • * Compliance: HIPAA, state regulations, CMS requirements-generic tools aren't built for this
  • * Integration: PointClickCare, Epic, Gehrimed-you can't rip and replace your EHR
  • * Reimbursement: PDPM, RAF, value-based care-software has to drive revenue, not just features
  • * Adoption: Clinical workflows, change management-technology is a people problem

We architect complex solutions that fit within your existing tech stack. Not another vendor to manage. Custom solutions that integrate with what you have-and create competitive advantage from your own data.
