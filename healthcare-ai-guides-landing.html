Why These Frameworks?

MIT's Project NANDA research (July 2025) found that 95% of enterprise AI initiatives deliver zero P&L impact. The 5% that succeed share common patterns: they start with back-office automation, they use custom solutions trained on organizational data, and they build incrementally.

These frameworks translate that research into actionable guidance. Each one answers the questions healthcare operators actually ask: Does this fit my organization? What data do I need? What's realistic ROI? When should I wait?

What the research shows:

  • 95% of AI pilots never reach production
  • 2x success rate for custom vs. internal builds
  • $2-10M annual savings from back-office automation
  • 18 mo window before market consolidation

The Honest Assessment

Not every organization should pursue these projects right now. Here's how to know.

Wait if:

  • ✗ Your billing data is fragmented or unreliable
  • ✗ You're mid-system migration
  • ✗ No one has authority to change workflows
  • ✗ You need results in 30 days

Move forward if:

  • ✓ You checked most boxes in the fit assessments
  • ✓ You have executive sponsorship
  • ✓ You can wait 90 days for measurable results
  • ✓ You're willing to change process, not just buy technology
