Quick Wins 8-14 weeks to deploy

Start Here: Revenue Cycle Quick Wins

These projects require only billing data exports—no EHR integration, no clinical documentation. They deliver measurable ROI within one quarter and build organizational confidence for larger investments.

8-14 weeks

A/R Collection Prioritization

AI-powered queue optimization that prioritizes accounts by collectability, not just age—reducing FTE time on uncollectible accounts.

Expected ROI: 5-15% bad debt reduction

 8-14 weeks

Claim Follow-Up Triage

Pattern matching that surfaces high-risk claims before they age out—prioritizing by urgency to past problems.

Expected ROI: 15-25% recovery per FTE

 10-16 weeks

Payer Contract Interpretation

AI that reads your contracts and flags underpayments—catching the carve-outs and escalators you're missing.

Expected ROI: 1-3% net collection lift
Building Momentum 12-22 weeks to deploy

Scale Your Success: Medium Complexity

These projects benefit from EHR integration and build on the data pipelines established by quick wins. They address scheduling, denial prevention, and coding accuracy.

12-18 weeks

No-Show Prediction

Predict which appointments will no-show—enabling smart overbooking and targeted intervention for high-risk slots.

Expected ROI: 20-40% no-show reduction

 14-20 weeks

Claim Denial Prediction

Predict which claims will deny before submission—with specific reason and fix recommendations.

Expected ROI: 25-50% denial write-off reduction

 16-22 weeks

Medical Coding Assistance

AI that reads clinical notes, suggests codes, and shows evidence—augmenting coders for accuracy and productivity.

Expected ROI: 20-35% coder productivity gain

 8-16 weeks

Coding Accuracy Pre-Check

Lightweight pattern matching that catches coding inconsistencies before submission—no EHR integration required.

Expected ROI: 10-20% coding denial reduction
Strategic Investments 20-32 weeks to deploy

Transform Operations: High Complexity

These projects require deep EHR integration and sustained organizational commitment. They deliver the largest ROI but should follow successful quick-win and medium-complexity projects.

20-28 weeks

Discharge Planning

Predict discharge dates 48+ hours in advance—giving care managers lead time to prevent avoidable bed days.

Expected ROI: 0.3-0.5 day LOS reduction

 22-30 weeks

CDI Auditor

AI that reviews every encounter for documentation improvement—capturing revenue that traditional CDI sampling misses.

Expected ROI: 2-5% CMI improvement

 24-32 weeks

Clinical Trial Matching

Continuously screen your patient population against trial criteria—capturing enrollment that manual screening misses.

Expected ROI: 30-50% enrollment increase
