FAQ

Common questions about telemedicine development.

Why build custom telemedicine instead of buying?

Off-the-shelf platforms force you to adapt your workflows to the technology. Custom telemedicine integrates with your existing systems, supports your specific care models, and scales with your organization. The investment pays off in adoption and outcomes.

How do you ensure HIPAA compliance?

We build HIPAA compliance into the architecture from day one—encrypted video, secure data storage, audit logging, and BAA-ready infrastructure. We've been doing this since 2007.

What about multi-state licensing?

Our platforms support multi-state provider networks with license verification, scheduling rules by state, and compliance tracking. NeverAlone operates across 7 states with appropriate licensing infrastructure.

How long does implementation take?

Typical telemedicine platform development takes 4-8 months depending on scope, integrations, and customization needs. We can often deliver MVP functionality faster and iterate from there.

What ROI can we expect?

Verified outcomes include 96% treat-in-place rates (avoiding $1,500+ ER visits), $2M+ in quality incentives, and expanded service reach. All financial analyst calculated, not vendor estimates.

What EHR systems do you integrate with?

Production integrations with PointClickCare, Epic, Gehrimed, Netsmart, and custom EHRs. Bidirectional, real-time—patient data flows in, documentation flows back.
