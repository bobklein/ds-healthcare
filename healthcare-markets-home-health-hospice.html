Market Challenges

The pressures facing home health and hospice

Documentation Burden

OASIS assessments, visit notes, care plans, medication reconciliation-clinicians spend 45+ minutes per visit on paperwork instead of patient care.

Clinician Capacity

Severe clinician shortages limit growth. Agencies need to see more patients with existing staff-which requires eliminating inefficiencies.

Scheduling Complexity

Balancing patient needs, clinician skills, geography, and availability is complex. Poor scheduling wastes drive time and creates missed visits.

OASIS Accuracy

OASIS drives reimbursement and quality scores. Inaccurate assessments leave money on the table and create compliance risk.

No-Show Management

Patient no-shows waste clinician time and reduce revenue. Proactive prediction and management can recover significant capacity.

Care Coordination

Coordinating care across visits, communicating with physicians, and managing transitions requires efficient workflows and visibility.
